Category: Dance


Words In Motion

"Where a dancer stands is holy ground. As long as Julius Caesar isn't lying dead next to you."

Take your creativity and dance skills to the next level by portraying British poems and plays with an Indian touch.

1) Number of teams per school - 1
2) Number of participants per team - 5 to 8
3) Styles of Dance- Bollywood, Semi-Classical, Classical and Indian Contemporary.

Round 1 -
The teams must perform a 3 minute dance, summarising the theme of any lyrical poetry of their choice.

Round 2 -
Qualifying teams must perform a 4 minute dance, portraying the plot of any one of Shakespeare's famous plays.

Event heads -
