Switch-A-Sketch

"Art is too important not to share."
~ Romero Britto

Explore the boundaries of your artistic creativity with Skitch-A-Sketch, an event that seamlessly combines art with science.

Participants will be provided with abstract scientific themes through a simple quiz, which they must potray in their own unique way.

Participants will have to work on two separate drawings simultaneously which they will switch at regular intervals.

2) Number of participants per team - 2
