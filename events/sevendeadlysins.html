7 Deadly Sins

Register

“The body remembers. Stuffed until an event, a sound, a sight, a touch, a word or a person awakens them.”

We bring to you '7 Deadly Sins', an event that provides teams a dance floor to delve into the depths of the human psyche.

1) Number of teams per school - 1
2) Number of participants per team - 2
3) Style of Dance - Any western dance form will be accepted (eg. Hip-hop, contemporary, jazz, modern etc.)
4) The team will be given a theme 2 days prior to the event.
5) Teams are required to prepare a 3 + 1 minute dance on the theme given and present it on the day of the fest.
6) Registration for this event is based on first-come-first-serve basis.
