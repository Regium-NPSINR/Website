Antiquity

“I want to make art, Artie. And I want to make trouble. You in?”
~ Cruella

1) Number of teams per school - 1
2) Number of participants per team - 6 (4 models + 2 designers)

Round 1 -
1) Each team will have to submit 3 concept designs online on the topic - mythical creatures.
2) Teams will have to incorporate 3D elements such as cloth, thermo-col etc. into their sketches.

Round 2 -
Participants will be given time to prepare their outfits beforehand and will have to walk the ramp on the day of the fest.
